BILE NERA

by NikTheWik
ENTER THE VOID

ABOUT

This project is about melancholy, the loneliness that assails us at the end of a relationship (in particular a cohabitation), the memory of something that was and that today is no longer. I wanted to investigate these feelings through everyday life. After a break up, the objects and the actions that are part of the everyday life take on other meanings because they are full of memories lived.
