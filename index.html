ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Nicola Pavoni. Welcome to my space!

I'm passionate about technology, films, videomaking, photography, video games, mangas and... Wait for it... Dungeons&Dragons!

In short, I'm a sensible nerd and a creative tinkerer. But if I think too much about it, I’m also an overthinker.

I’m curious by nature, I love mysteries and everything to do with space.

I've worked for 2 years in the film industry as Assistant Film Editor. But then I decided to move to something more creative and stable. So now I'm studying to become Front-End Developer at start2impact University.

NikTheWik is a sort of alter ego through which I express myself. Whether it's a website, a video or a photo it doesn't matter: NikTheWik will take care of it.

From Another World describes the source of my inspiration, my style. All my works present a dreamy and unique atmosphere that seems to have distant spacey origins.
